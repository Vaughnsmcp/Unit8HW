About Me

Vaughn recently moved from Denver with his partner, Daniel. There, he was a prominent member of the local music scene performing with varied acts. His genres include everything from classical piano to folk rock to psyche-pop. He spent most of his free time playing out, touring back and forth from coast to coast and in Europe, and recording his own projects as well studio sessions for others.

Vaughn became interested in coding as he prepares for life's next chapter. He has always been a videogame fan, and originally went to school for classical performace and composition with the goal to make original soundtracks for RPGs. He has recently begun working with a friend and amature coder on a DIY game, and coding seemed like a natural step. He's also really, really, really sick of waiting tables.
